A Paradigm Shift in Tax Software

Tax preparation doesn't have to feel like a casino. Here is how TAXEM does things differently.

Frequently Asked Questions

Learn more about our local-first architecture and civic principles.

Is TAXEM online or offline?

It is designed to run entirely locally. The website you are viewing now is a promo showcase, but the actual TAXEM engine compiles and runs on your machine. Your financial information never uploads to our servers.

Why do you emphasize Quebec?

Quebec has a separate, highly distinct provincial tax filing system. Most software treats it as an afterthought. We built our architecture to natively support dual-jurisdiction coupling from the ground up.

Is it certified by the CRA and Revenu Québec?

This is currently a prototype and promo showcase for the 2024 and 2025 tax calculations. We are actively organizing reference materials and structuring calculations to prepare for formal certification cycles.

Will it always be free from upsells?

Yes. The platform is built on civic tech principles. There will never be "paywalls for deductions" or unexpected upgrade fees. We charge a flat fee for the executable package, or you can build it from source.
